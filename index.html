Background

I’m a guy who enjoys doing a lot of things. I've traveled a lot which led to me living in Japan for about four years. While there I picked up the language and a love for trying to make video games! After developing a few mediocre games that people could play in their browser I began to take an interest in developing web apps.

Now I've returned to the States to finish school and teach myself how to be a better web designer! I'm very excited to expand my skills and see what I can accomplish.

  • HTML

  • Ruby

The ultimate goal is to work as a developer who creates websites that makes the end user not have to think. Or if he does think, he thinks "Hmm. What a great website."
